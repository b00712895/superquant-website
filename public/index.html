合格投资者认定Qualified Investor Confirmation

重要提示：在继续浏览本网站前，请确认您或您所代表的机构符合“合格投资者”标准，并仔细阅读以下说明。

一、合格投资者标准

合格投资者应具备相应的风险识别能力和风险承担能力，投资于单只私募基金的金额不低于人民币100万元，并符合下列标准之一：

  1. 净资产不低于人民币1,000万元的单位；
  2. 金融资产不低于人民币300万元，或者最近三年个人年均收入不低于人民币50万元的个人。

二、视为合格投资者的情形

以下投资者视为合格投资者：

  1. 社会保障基金、企业年金等养老基金，以及慈善基金等社会公益基金；
  2. 依法设立并在中国证券投资基金业协会备案的投资计划；
  3. 投资于所管理私募基金的私募基金管理人及其从业人员；
  4. 中国证监会规定的其他投资者。

Important Notice: Before continuing to this website, please confirm that you or the institution you represent meets the criteria for a “qualified investor” and read the following notice carefully.

1. Qualified Investor Criteria

A qualified investor must have the ability to identify and bear investment risks, invest no less than RMB 1 million in a single private fund, and meet one of the following criteria:

  1. an institution with net assets of no less than RMB 10 million; or
  2. an individual with financial assets of no less than RMB 3 million, or average annual personal income of no less than RMB 500,000 over the preceding three years.

2. Investors Deemed to Be Qualified Investors

The following investors are deemed to be qualified investors:

  1. social security funds, enterprise annuities and other pension funds, as well as charitable funds and other public welfare funds;
  2. investment plans legally established and filed with the Asset Management Association of China;
  3. private fund managers and their employees investing in private funds under their management; and
  4. other investors specified by the China Securities Regulatory Commission.
